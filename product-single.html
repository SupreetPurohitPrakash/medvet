Dalguban N Plus Oil Vaccine

Composition

It contains inactivated Newcastle disease virus (KBNP-C4152R2L strain) propagated in 10 day-old hen's eggs and oil as adjuvant (ISA 70)

Indications

For vaccination of health chickens, as an aid in the preventing Newcastle disease.(the vaccine is indicated for the vaccination of breeding an dlaying type chicken focks pre-immunized aganst Newcastle disease with live virus vaccines.)

Dosage and Administration

Vaccinate birds with 0.5 ml of the vaccine intramuscularly.

Storage

Store at 2 to 10°C and protect from light.

Cautions

Vaccinate healthy birds only.

Before use, it is advisable to keep the vaccine at room temperature for some hours up to 15-25°C. Shake the vaccine befroe use.
