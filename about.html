About Us

Medvet Pvt. Ltd.

A privately-held Nepal-based Importing & Marketing company

Medvet Pharma Pvt. Ltd. is a company established in 2011 AD with the purpose of serving animal health sector by professionals who have an experience of more than 2 decades in veterinary medicines and animal health. Medvet Pharma is an import based company which is affiliated and partnering with several multi-national companies across the globe.

With a purpose to serve the livestock sector of Nepal, Medvet Pharma has been importing high quality veterinary pharmaceuticals , veterinary vaccines , nutraceuticals and feed premixes which has been accredited by WHO GMP , EU GMP , FAMI Q’s and VMD UK.
